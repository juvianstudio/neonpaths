Data Safety Summary

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety standards, we disclose the following collection and sharing practices:

Data Type Collection Purpose Encryption
Device IDs Collected & Shared Advertising (AdMob) HTTPS/TLS
App Activity Collected Analytics & Performance HTTPS/TLS

What We Collect

  • Game Progress Data: Level completion, star ratings, move counts, and puzzle history — stored locally on your device and, if applicable, synced via Google Play Games Services.
  • Daily Puzzle Streak Data: To track and display your daily puzzle participation streak.
  • Analytics Data (Anonymous): Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics such as session length, crash reports, and feature usage — collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Advertising ID (Device ID): Used by AdMob for ad personalization and measurement, subject to your device settings.
  • Device Information: OS version, device model, and language settings — used for compatibility and localization.

NeonPaths does not require account creation. No name, email address, or payment information is directly collected by us.

How We Use Your Data

  • To save and sync your game progress across sessions.
  • To operate the Daily Puzzle feature and maintain streaks.
  • To analyze and improve gameplay performance and stability.
  • To serve advertisements via AdMob and measure ad effectiveness.
  • To provide multilingual support tailored to your device's language settings.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Games Services – cloud save and leaderboards.
  • Google Firebase / Analytics – crash reporting and anonymous usage analytics.
  • Google AdMob – displaying advertisements and collecting Device IDs for ad targeting.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

NeonPaths does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that data from our records.

Rights & Compliance

Users in the EU (GDPR) and California (CCPA) may exercise their rights to access or delete data. Since we do not maintain a personal user database, these rights are primarily exercised through device Advertising ID resets and Google Account settings.

Data Retention

Local game data is retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data. Anonymized analytics data on third-party servers is kept no longer than 24 months.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the version number and review date above.

Delete Your Data

Juvian Studio · NeonPaths · Android

Request Account Data Removal

Because NeonPaths does not store user data on private servers, 'deletion' refers to the removal of local device data and the dissociation of your Advertising ID from third-party services like AdMob.

To request a manual reset or instructions on how to dissociate your identifiers from third-party services, please email us from your registered account.

✉ [email protected]

Local Data Deletion

You can delete all local game progress and cached data instantly by navigating to your device's Settings → Apps → NeonPaths → Storage → Clear Data.

Uninstalling the App will also permanently remove all locally stored data from your device.

Advertising ID Reset

To dissociate your device from AdMob ad targeting, you can reset your Advertising ID at any time:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID (Android).
  • You can also opt out of personalized ads entirely from the same menu.

What Gets Removed

  • All locally stored game progress, level completions, and star ratings.
  • Daily puzzle streak records on your device.
  • Your Advertising ID association with AdMob (via ID reset).
  • Any Google Play Games cloud save data (managed via your Google Account).

Processing Timeline

We aim to acknowledge email requests within 5 business days and complete any server-side dissociation within 30 days. Local data deletion via device settings is immediate.
