Privacy Policy

Version: 1.0.3 | Last Reviewed: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the data practices of the NeonPaths mobile application, developed by Juvian Studio. NeonPaths is a standalone puzzle game — we do not operate any servers, maintain any user database, or collect any personal information from our users. All game data is stored locally on your device. The only third-party service integrated into the App is Google AdMob for displaying ads, which operates independently under Google's own privacy policy.

What Juvian Studio Does NOT Do

  • We do not operate any servers or databases.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • We do not use Firebase, analytics SDKs, or any crash reporting tools.
  • We do not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any identifiable information.
  • We do not sell, share, or trade any user data — because we have none to share.

How Game Data is Stored

All game data — including level completions, star ratings, game settings, and daily puzzle streaks — is stored exclusively on your local device using the shared_preferences package. This data is never transmitted to any server, never shared with any third party, and is only accessible by the App on your device.

Juvian Studio has no access to this data at any time.

You can delete this data at any time by going to Settings → Apps → NeonPaths → Storage → Clear Data, or by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

NeonPaths displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob operates independently of the App and connects directly to Google's ad servers — Juvian Studio does not receive, process, or have access to any of this data. AdMob may collect the following automatically:

  • Advertising ID: Your device's Advertising ID is used to serve and personalize ads.
  • Diagnostic Information: Google AdMob may collect crash logs, app launch time, and performance data to ensure ad stability.
  • User Interactions: Information regarding how you interact with ads (taps, views) is collected by Google to measure ad performance.

For details on how Google handles this data, please review the Google Privacy Policy ↗.

There are no other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or tracking libraries in NeonPaths.

Advertising & Opt-Out

You can control ad personalization at any time directly on your Android device:

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Reset your Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID

You may still see non-personalized (contextual) ads after opting out. There is currently no in-app purchase or option to remove ads.

Children's Privacy

NeonPaths is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any user data directly, there is no risk of inadvertent personal data collection on our part. However, Google AdMob is subject to its own child-directed content policies. If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, please contact us at juvianstudio@gmail.com.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since Juvian Studio does not collect or hold any personal data, there is no personal data on our end to access, correct, or delete. For data handled by Google AdMob, your rights are exercised directly with Google:

  • Reset or opt out of your Advertising ID via Android device settings.
  • Manage your Google Account data at myaccount.google.com ↗.
  • Contact us at juvianstudio@gmail.com with any questions and we will direct you accordingly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the version number and review date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
